Latitude vs Cloudiness: Summary

Analysis

In the Cloudiness visualization we can see almost no trend with latitude. What is interesting however, is that the ciites appear to form lines at certian percentages; notably 0% cloudiness, 20% cloudiness, and around 75% cloudiness. This, I assume, has to do with the way we record the data. This is to say that these cities correlation to specfic cloud coverage percentages is likely an artifact of data collection/entry, rather than the an accurate representation of the 'true nature' of the cloud coverage. More research into how this API collects data would be needed to find answers on that point.
